What are Perfect Numbers?

A perfect number is a positive integer that equals the sum of its proper divisors (excluding itself). These rare mathematical gems have fascinated mathematicians for over 2,000 years!

Euclid-Euler Theorem

Every even perfect number can be represented as:

Perfect Number = 2p-1 √ó (2p - 1)

where 2p - 1 is a Mersenne prime

This elegant formula, discovered by Euclid and proven complete by Euler, connects perfect numbers to Mersenne primes. Finding Mersenne primes allows us to generate new perfect numbers!
